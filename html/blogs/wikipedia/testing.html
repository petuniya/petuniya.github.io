Who is a Cat?

The cat (Felis catus) is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal..

It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is commonly referred to as the domestic cat or house cat to distinguish it from the wild members of the family..

How many types are there??

Currently, there are about 600 million domestic cats in the world, about 200 breeds have been bred, from long-haired (Persian cats) to hairless (Sphynx cats), recognized and registered by various felinological organizations..
